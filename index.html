About me

I am currently a research associate at NYU Abu Dhabi (NYUAD) and a lecturer at Sungkyunkwan University (SKKU). My applied research focuses on the rapidly evolving dynamics of digital media platforms, pulling from diverse areas to study human-machine social behavior, with emphasis on social information and language processing. I teach DSC3025 Applied Natural Language Processing (NLP) at SKKU.

I recently graduated from Drexel University College of Computing & Informatics with a PhD in Information Science advised by Dr. Tony Hu. My research centers around deriving insights from digital media data focusing on social information and language processing. I collaborate over work engaging a broad range of areas, including sociology, computer science, health and clinical informatics, communications and political science.

I love travel and photography.
